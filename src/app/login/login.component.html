How it works

We all sometimes purchase shoes that do not fit - either to resell or accident but most commonly to trade for a size that we want.

Add sneakers to your virtual closest that you wish to trade

Select sneakrs that you are willing to trade in exchange for one of your sneakers

Sit back and relax! We will connect you with someone who is willing to trade

It is then up to you to arrange the trade. It is strongly suggested that you read the guide to safe trading

Many trades will require shipment to different states or even countries. Each person pays their own shipping
